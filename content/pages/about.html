Simon Tennant

Simon grew up in South Africa and moved to the UK to study computer science at Warwick University. His first venture: A company providing internet infrastructure for banks like UBS, Deutsche Bank in London. Then, to gain first-hand experience of working for fast growing companies, Simon moved to Silicon Valley and worked for Winamp/Spinner/AOL-music and a small Linux-based startup that raised $90M.

Simon sits on XMPP Standards Foundation board, runs marathons and climbs mountains.
