Privacy 2.0

For Organisations:

Keep information belonging to you or your team secure by running your own Buddycloud server inside your organisation's network. You control which information you share outside of the organisation. Your organization's communication data stays private.

For Developers:

Protect users' privacy with Buddycloud's secure design and inspectable open source code and protocol.

For Users:
